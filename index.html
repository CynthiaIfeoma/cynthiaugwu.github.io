About Me

I am a Strategic Intelligence Analyst with a passion for uncovering hidden patterns in complex global data. My work sits at the intersection of technology, geopolitics, and corporate security.

With my Cambridge MPhil in Criminological Research and extensive field experience across MENA, Sahel, and European regions, I bring both academic rigor and practical expertise to every project.

I specialise in OSINT investigations, geopolitical risk analysis, threat intelligence, compliance, and due diligence - helping organisations navigate an increasingly complex risk landscape. My automated intelligence frameworks have reduced client risk exposure by up to 45% while improving operational efficiency.

When I'm not analysing geopolitical trends or building intelligence dashboards, you will find me writing about emerging threats, mentoring junior analysts, or exploring new OSINT techniques and tools.
